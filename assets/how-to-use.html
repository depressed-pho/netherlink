Creating portals

To create a portal in the Overworld, enter coordinates in the bottom left corner and click the icon at the Overworld portal list on the left side of the screen.

Maps

You can also adjust X and Z coordinates by scrolling maps with your mouse of fingers. The map on the left side is for the Overworld, and the right side is for the Nether. You can also change the scale of maps with your mouse wheel or by pinching.

The maps show two things: portals in the same dimension denoted by small circles, and search ranges of portals in the opposite dimension denoted by squares. A search range of a portal is where to look for a destination portal to establish a linkage. The small symbol in the search range denotes the nominal destination of the portal.

Renaming and deleting portals

To delete a portal, select a portal in the portal list and click the icon . By clicking the icon you can rename or change the color of a portal.

Managing worlds

Netherlink can handle more than one world. You can create a new world by clicking the button " New..." on top of the screen, and switch worlds with the world selector on the top left corner of the screen. The button " Delete..." deletes a world, and the button " Rename..." renames a world.

To export a world to a local file, click the button " Export...". By clicking the button " Import..." you can import a saved file to the application.
